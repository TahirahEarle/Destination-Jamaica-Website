ABOUT US

Destination Jamaica was founded in the year 1990 as a part of the Jamican Tourism Associaton, by three entrepreneurs and travel enthusiast: Michele Kerr, Anna Parade and Aisha Liaham. Like you, we are travellers. It is in our blood, who we are and what we do everyday. As Jamaicans, tourism has been an integral part in our country’s development. We are passionate about discovering a new way to travel around the world in style and being deeply committed to the idea of sustainably exploring this incredible world as part of a global community. Woven together through rich cultures, histories, wildlife and a natural beauty; our planet, our home, is place to embrace and discover wholeheartedly. We create once in a life time experiences that offer life changing moments and help to help you and your love ones to fulfil that deep seated urge for connection and learning. This company offers tours journeying from the Beautiful Island of Jamaica and carters to the entire world who is waiting to explore Jamicas, just as how a Yaardie would..

Mission Statement

Our Mission is to help people travel to Jamiaca safer and smarter, in order that they achieve all the experience a trip has to offer. So they could experience a diffent view, which we bring with our different voices, backgrounds and perspectives form our Melting Pot Culture. To treat everyone with respect, dignity and fairness. To empower each person to develop their Confident Self to the fullest. We’re committed to making a positive impact in the lives of others.
